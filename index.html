    Our History

    Nurse Asante is a private nursing agency founded with a deep commitment to delivering professional, personalized healthcare services in the comfort of clients’ homes. Led by Nurse Asante herself, the agency was born out of a passion to provide the same standard of care she would want for her own loved ones. With a focus on respect, dignity, and clinical excellence, Nurse Asante and her team offer a wide range of nursing and support services tailored to each individual’s medical and personal needs—while prioritizing safety, autonomy, and compassionate care.

    Our Mission

    "Empowering Health Equality through Compassionate Nurse Home Visits"

    Our nursing home visiting team is dedicated to bridging the gap in healthcare access and outcomes for vulnerable populations in the UK. W e provide personalized, high-quality care and support to individuals, promoting health equity, autonomy and dignity for all.

    Our vision is that we will define and deliver best practices to care for our loved ones and set the standard for compassion and innovation.
